About Us

Your trusted partner for professional dry cleaning services

MAG DRY CLEANERS is a premier dry cleaning service provider located in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. Established on September 1st, 2025, we have quickly become known for our exceptional quality, attention to detail, and customer-centric approach.

Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to providing the highest quality dry cleaning services for all types of garments and fabrics. We use state-of-the-art equipment and eco-friendly cleaning solutions to ensure your clothes are not only clean but also well-preserved.

Located on Bahari Beach Road, we serve both individual and commercial clients, offering convenient pickup and delivery services to make your life easier.
